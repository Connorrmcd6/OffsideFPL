Where do I find my Team ID?
  1. Login to your FPL account here on a browser of your choice.
  2. Click on the "Pick Team" tab near the top of the page.
  3. Click on "Gameweek History". This will be to the right of your screen on PC and towards the bottom of the screen on mobile.
  4. Once that page loads, check the URL in your browser address bar. The number in the URL is your Team ID.
